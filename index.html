Welcome To
No Boundaries.

Balancing children's wellbeing through cricket and exercise.

We provide sessions using various cricket and exercise based activities to promote and improve a child’s physical, mental and social wellbeing. By encouraging and increasing these three areas of wellbeing, a child has the best chance to thrive in all areas of their future lives and together we can show them they have No Boundaries.

At No Boundaries we work with Primary Schools across London and Surrey. We run after school clubs, holiday camps and Saturday sessions. We are an expierenced and passionate team who work closely with the schools whilst delivering sessions.

We are led by directors Adam London, an ex professional cricketer for over six years, alongside Fitness coach Nick Hampshire, who has worked across the country delievering exercise plans and sessions to schools and clubs as well as businesses.
