Introduction

At No Boundaries we work with Primary Schools across London and Surrey. We run after school clubs, holiday camps and Saturday sessions. We are ran by an expierenced and passionate team who work closely with the schools whilst delivering sessions.

We are led by directors Adam London, an ex professional cricketer for over six years, alongside Fitness coach Nick Hampshire, who has worked across the country delievering exercise plans and sessions to schools, clubs as well as businesses.
